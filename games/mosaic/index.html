Restore the floor.

An 8×8 mosaic lies under the dust, and the numbers know where. Lay every tessera exactly where it belongs — the clock is your score, lower sits higher on Olympus.

  • 3 1 Clues give the runs of consecutive tiles in each row and column, in order, with at least one gap between runs. 0 means a bare line.
  • TILE Lays tesserae. Drag to paint a whole stroke; tap a laid tile to lift it.
  • MARK Notes a confirmed-empty cell with a dim ×. Notes are for you — only tiles count.
  • R·CLK On desktop, right-click or right-drag applies the other tool.
  • SOLVE Stuck? SOLVE reveals the finished floor — but a mosaic laid by the gods earns no score.
